Frequently Asked Questions

Creators

How much of the event revenue do I get?
95%. Our goal is to support you and your community, so we want to stay out of your way. As a result, we take 5%, so that we can cover fees associated with online transactions.
How am I protected from toxic fans?
We have a block function that prevents someone from seeing any of your events. You may also remove a fan from any event for behavior you deem inappropriate or abusive in any way.
When does someone get a refund? Can I dispute it?
Fans must file a complaint first. We’ll then contact you to review the situation. If you left or cancelled the event without reason or abused the fan in some way, we’ll have to issue a refund.
If someone gets a refund does everyone else get one too?
Yes. If only one refund was issued, it would open up opportunities to exploit events.
What if someone lies and says I didn’t show up?
We’ll investigate first. Recording your session in some way provides clean proof of your actions.

Fans

How am I protected from toxic creators?
We have a block function that prevents someone from seeing any of your events. You may also remove a fan from any event for behavior you deem inappropriate or abusive in any way.
How do I request a refund?
Send us an email at customersupport@apogee.gg titled “Refund Request“ from the email associated with your account and we will investigate your claim.
How do I connect with the creator once I’ve joined and event?
We have set up an instant messaging service on the event itself. Once you’ve won the event, you and the creator can message back and forth to discuss how to connect.
